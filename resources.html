Our Best-Selling Bible Resources

The Genesis Project

Embark on a transformative journey with 'The Genesis Project,' a Bible Activity Workbook filled with engaging Bible stories, application questions, puzzles, and activities designed to disciple children. Perfect for Sunday schools, churches, and homes, this resource fosters intentional engagement with Scripture for $10.

The Son of Favor

'The Son of Favor' is a Customized Bible Activity Workbook that connects seamlessly to specific Bible curricula used by churches or schools, helping children engage effectively and intentionally with Bible lessons. Tailored for spiritual growth, this special textbook is available for $12, ideal for targeted discipleship.

The Friend of Abraham

'The Friend of Abraham' is a Custom Bible Curriculum designed to assist Children’s Church workers and teachers in delivering intentional Sunday Bible lessons. With customizable content tailored to your church or school’s needs, this resource ensures impactful teaching for $15, fostering deep biblical understanding.

The Genesis Project Coloring Book

'The Genesis Project Coloring Book' features vibrant illustrations of Bible stories, perfect for kids to enjoy as a follow-up activity in church, schools, or at home. This engaging resource, priced at $8, encourages creative interaction with Scripture, enhancing children’s spiritual journey.

The Heart of Courage

'The Heart of Courage' is a special themed Bible Stories book for kids, featuring inspiring tales of David, Mary, Joshua, and Esther. Designed for school libraries, this resource, priced at $12, instills courage and faith, making it an excellent addition to any child’s reading collection.

The First Family

'The First Family' is a Christian Religious Education (CRE) Textbook focused on helping students interact personally with the Bible, tailored to their cultural context. This innovative resource, priced at $14, encourages deep, meaningful engagement with Scripture for classroom use.

My God, My Country, and Myself

'My God, My Country, and Myself' is a special themed Bible storybook that explores faith, identity, and community through captivating narratives for children. Priced at $13, this book is ideal for school libraries and home reading, fostering spiritual growth and cultural connection.
