About Chazak Africa

Our Mission to Empower Children Through the Bible

Learn about our vision, team, and commitment to discipling the next generation across Africa.

Discover Our Story

Who We Are

Chazak Africa is a strategic Christian literature resource company dedicated to transforming and discipling Africa for Christ. We aim to develop and produce world-class literature resources that benefit the young generation in Africa and beyond, fostering faith and spiritual growth through engaging, contextually relevant materials.

Our Vision

To play a strong part in the transformation of the world through our strategic business activities, creating impactful Bible-based resources that inspire children and educators alike. We envision a future where every child in Africa experiences the Bible as a unified story that leads to Jesus.

Our Mission

To be a business enterprise that endeavors to meet every need of our customers, providing high-quality, intentional, and transformative Bible resources for children’s discipleship. Our mission drives us to create resources that foster faith, engage kids creatively, and support educators in discipling the next generation effectively.

Why Choose Our Services?

  • We are strategic in our approach, capturing the core elements of our customers' needs.
  • We are very mindful of our customers' financial budgets, ensuring affordability and value.
  • We aim for the best possible performance to enhance our customers' experience.
  • We go the extra mile in all our operations, delivering excellence in every resource we create.

Our Core Values

  • Efficiency: We optimize our processes to deliver timely, high-quality resources.
  • Accountability: We take responsibility for our commitments and outcomes.
  • Proactive: We anticipate needs and innovate proactively to meet them.
  • Creativity: We design engaging, imaginative materials that captivate children.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our resources to stay ahead in children’s ministry.
  • Hard Work: We dedicate ourselves fully to transforming lives through Scripture.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of educators, writers, and ministry leaders brings decades of experience in children’s discipleship and biblical education. Based in Africa, we collaborate with churches, schools, and families to ensure our resources resonate with local cultures and needs. Meet some of our key members:

  • Jane Kinyanjui – Founder & CEO, with over 15 years of experience in Christian education.
  • Michael Omondi – Creative Director, specializing in engaging Bible story illustrations for kids.
  • Sarah Wanjiru – Curriculum Developer, ensuring our materials align with biblical truth and educational standards.

Our Impact

Since our founding, Chazak Africa has reached over 500,000 children across Africa with our Bible resources, empowering churches and schools to disciple the next generation. Our work has been praised by pastors, educators, and parents for its transformative impact on young lives.
