What is Chazak Africa?

Chazak Africa is a strategic Christian literature resource company dedicated to transforming and discipling Africa for Christ through world-class resources for kids. We aim to meet the needs of churches, schools, and families with affordable, impactful materials that inspire faith and spiritual growth, tailored to the African context.
